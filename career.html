How to Apply

Application Process

Simple and transparent — no lengthy forms, no gatekeepers.

STEP 01

Send Your CV

Email your CV to careers@redcreative.mv with the role in the subject. Include a brief intro about yourself.

STEP 02

Portfolio Review

We review your portfolio and assess your fit for the role. You may be asked for a short trial task.

STEP 03

Interview

A relaxed conversation about your experience, goals, and what you'd bring to the team.

STEP 04

Offer Letter

If we're aligned, you'll receive a formal offer with compensation details and start date.

STEP 05

Onboarding

Structured orientation, equipment training, and buddy system from day one.
