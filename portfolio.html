Common Questions

Portfolio FAQ

Can I see physical samples of your work?

+
Yes! Visit our Hulhumalé workshop to see material samples, finish options, and examples of our work across different categories. We can also send sample kits to other islands for a small shipping fee.

Do you work with international clients?

+
While most of our clients are Maldives-based, we have worked with international resort chains and businesses operating here. All coordination can be done remotely via email and WhatsApp.

Can I get a project similar to one in the portfolio?

+
Absolutely! Share the portfolio reference with your requirements and we'll provide a tailored quote. We can replicate, modify, or improve upon any shown project to match your specific needs and budget.

Do you have more projects not shown here?

+
Yes — this is a curated selection. With 5000+ completed projects, we can share category-specific portfolios (resorts, retail, elections, etc.) on request. Just ask and we'll send relevant examples.

What's the typical budget range for projects shown?

+
Budgets vary widely — from MVR 500 for small sticker orders to MVR 500,000+ for full resort signage packages. Every project is custom-quoted based on materials, size, quantity, and complexity.
