Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to questions we get often.

How quickly can I get a quote?

+
We typically respond within 24 hours. For urgent requests, WhatsApp is the fastest — we usually reply within minutes during business hours.

Do you deliver to other islands?

+
Yes! We deliver across the Maldives. Delivery times and costs vary by island. Contact us with your location for a delivery estimate — we partner with reliable cargo and speed boat services.

Can I visit your studio without an appointment?

+
Absolutely. Walk-ins are welcome during our business hours (Sun–Thu, 9AM–6PM). However, for large projects or consultations, we recommend scheduling an appointment so we can prepare samples and dedicate proper time to your project.

What file formats do you accept for printing?

+
We accept PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), PSD (Photoshop), EPS, SVG, and high-resolution PNG/JPEG (300 DPI minimum). If you're unsure about your file, send it over and our design team will check it for print readiness at no extra charge.

Do you offer design services as well?

+
Yes! We have an in-house design team that can create designs from scratch or refine your existing concepts. Design services are available at competitive rates. Just share your idea or reference images and we'll take care of the rest.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept cash, bank transfer, and mobile payment (Ooredoo M-Faisaa / Dhiraagu Pay). For corporate clients, we also offer invoice-based payment terms. A 50% advance is typically required before production begins.
