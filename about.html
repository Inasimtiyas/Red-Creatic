About Red Creative

Building the Future of Print in the Maldives

Founded by Hassan Zahir in Hulhumalé

Red Creative started with a simple belief: the Maldives deserves world-class printing and fabrication services without having to look overseas. What began as a small print shop with two machines has grown into the country's most comprehensive printing, signage, and 3D fabrication facility.

Today, with 25+ team members, cutting-edge equipment, and over 5,000 completed projects, we serve everyone from local businesses to international resort chains — all from our Hulhumalé headquarters.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2018

The Beginning

Red Creative founded in Hulhumalé with 2 printers and a small team of 4. Started with basic sticker and poster printing services.

2019

First Major Client

Secured our first resort signage contract. Invested in large-format printers and hired our first designer. Client count crossed 100.

2020

Resilience Through COVID

Pivoted to safety signage and social distancing materials. Kept the team intact and supported the national pandemic response.

2021

CNC & Laser Added

Expanded into fabrication with CNC router and CO2 laser cutter. Added signboard production capabilities. Team grew to 15.

2022

3D Printing Revolution

Launched the Maldives' most advanced 3D printing facility. Multi-material FDM and resin capabilities put us on the map nationally.

2023

1000 Clients Milestone

Crossed 1,000 clients and 3,000 projects. Expanded workshop to 5,000 sq ft. Started island-wide delivery service.

2024

Election Campaign Leader

Became the go-to partner for election campaign materials. Handled 8+ atoll-wide campaigns simultaneously. Team reached 25+.

2025

The Future

5,000+ projects completed. Expanding into UV flatbed printing and exploring new fabrication technologies. The journey continues.

Common Questions

About FAQ

Where is Red Creative located?

+
We're located in Hulhumalé, Malé Atoll, Maldives. Our workshop is easily accessible from Malé via ferry or road. Walk-ins are welcome during business hours (Sun–Thu, 9AM–6PM).

Are you a registered Maldivian business?

+
Yes, Red Creative is a fully registered business in the Republic of Maldives. We operate under proper business registration, tax compliance, and all required trade licenses.

What makes you different from other printers?

+
Three key things: (1) End-to-end capability — design to installation all in-house, (2) The Maldives' most advanced 3D printing facility, and (3) Island-wide delivery to any atoll. Most printers in Maldives only do one part of the process.

Do you work with government clients?

+
Yes. We've worked with multiple government agencies, ministries, and state-owned enterprises on signage, printing, and fabrication projects. We follow all required procurement processes.

Can I visit the workshop before placing an order?

+
Absolutely! We encourage visits. You can see our equipment, check material samples, meet the team, and discuss your project in person. Just contact us to arrange a convenient time.
