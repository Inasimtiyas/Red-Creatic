Common Questions

Service FAQ

What's your minimum order quantity?

+
We have no strict minimum — we handle everything from a single custom sticker to 100,000 labels. For small orders, pricing may be slightly higher per unit. Contact us for a quote regardless of quantity.

How long does production take?

+
Standard jobs: 2–5 working days. Rush orders can be done same-day or next-day depending on the service and quantity. 3D printing timelines depend on print size and complexity — we'll give you an exact timeline with your quote.

Can you design from a concept or reference?

+
Absolutely. Our in-house design team can create designs from scratch based on your brief, reference images, or even a rough sketch. Design services are charged separately or bundled with print orders at a discount.

What file formats do you accept?

+
We prefer vector formats: AI, EPS, SVG, PDF. For photos/raster: PNG, TIFF, or JPEG at 300 DPI minimum. If you're unsure, send what you have — we'll check print-readiness for free.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

+
Yes! We offer tiered pricing — the higher the quantity, the lower the per-unit cost. We also have special rates for repeat clients and long-term contracts. Ask about our corporate packages.

Can I see a sample before full production?

+
Yes, we provide digital proofs for approval before printing. For physical samples, a small fee may apply depending on the product. For large projects like signage, we can produce a section sample for on-site review.
